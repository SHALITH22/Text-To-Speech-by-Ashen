👋 Welcome — how to use

  1. Type or paste your text in the big box.
  2. Pick a mode at the top:
    • ⚡ Instant — starts speaking immediately using your device's built-in voices. No download. Best for quick reads.
    • 🎙️ Studio — natural neural voices you can download as audio. The first time, it downloads the voice model once (then it's cached and fast forever).
  3. Choose a ⭐ Most natural voice for the most human sound, then set Speed and Volume.
  4. Hit Speak / Generate. In Studio mode, press ⬇ Download WAV to save the audio.

💡 Speed tip: Studio mode is fastest on Chrome or Edge (uses your graphics card). The badge under the mode buttons shows whether you're on fast GPU or slower CPU mode.
